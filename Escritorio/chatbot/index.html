How to use ChatGPT?

1. Provide a prompt

Give the bot any piece of text for generating a response. It can be a question, a search query, a statement, a declaration, or a task. Any words basically.

2. Generate a response

After you have entered a prompt, press the send button, and wait a little bit for ChatGPT to generate a response.

3. Review the response

If you are not completely satisfied with the response (it is incomplete or inaccurate), you can repeat steps 1 and 2 to give the bot more context or to point out its inaccuracies.

4. Keep communicating

Keep asking questions about the topic you are interested in, take as much time as you need to clarify details. Or try chatting with ChatGPT about new topics to evaluate all its features.
